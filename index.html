About Hands of Gold

Built on craftsmanship, trust, and personal service.

Established in 1983, Hands of Gold has been a cornerstone of elegance and craftsmanship in the heart of Long Island. Hands of Gold combines traditional techniques with cutting-edge craftsmanship, ensuring that every piece reflects the highest standards of quality and beauty.

Families return to Hands of Gold for honest advice, dependable repair work, and custom jewelry created with real attention to detail. Every visit is handled with warmth, professionalism, and the kind of care that only a family-run business can offer.
